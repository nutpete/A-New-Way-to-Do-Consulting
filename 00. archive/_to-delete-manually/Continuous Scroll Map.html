▶ READ THIS AS

Tweak knob, not law. If a section feels too cold, dial pixel down. If it feels disconnected, increase what it inherits from above.

01

Paper holds until you earn space.

Don't switch backgrounds for a tonal beat. The Wait. moment, the Counter Approved stamp, even the receipt — they all sit on paper. Space arrives only when fluency arrives.

02

Bleed, don't cut.

When backgrounds change, do it as a 200–400px dither bridge with the previous color, then a chunk that's half-and-half, then full new. Never a hard horizontal line.

03

One motif migrates.

Each section sends a sprite ahead — a debris dot, a planet ring, a star. By the time you see it again two sections later, your eye recognizes it: same world, deeper into it.
