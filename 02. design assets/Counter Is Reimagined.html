▶ SPACE GRADIENT BLEED

Bottom-right corner

A radial gradient anchored at 110% 130% bleeds the journey's space color (#050810) into this section, so the .how dither bridge has somewhere to come from.

.popup-section::after { background: radial-gradient( ellipse 80% 80% at 110% 130%, var(--space) 0%, transparent 78%); }
▶ DITHER FOOT

2×2 checker, 36px tall

Same dither motif from the .how transition, dropped at the section's bottom edge as a 36px strip. When .how starts immediately below, the patterns line up — no visible seam.

.popup-section::before { bottom: 0; height: 36px; background-size: 16px 8px; }
▶ RECEIPT

Pixel Kit · Element 08

Header in Press Start 2P. Solid 2px ink border + two-tone stepped shadow (ink → fluency). Total row also Press Start 2P, with --fluency-3 on the result for a touch of arcade glow.

border:2px solid var(--ink); box-shadow: 4px 4px 0 var(--ink), 8px 8px 0 var(--fluency);
▶ AMBIENT SPRITES

4 small, ≤42px

Tiny pixel rocket, planet (with rings), satellite, and a 4-arm star. Scatter at low opacity behind copy. They preview the journey's iconography without spoiling the reveal.

image-rendering: pixelated; shape-rendering: crispEdges; opacity: 0.85;
▶ COMET + ORBIT

Decorative around receipt

A clipped pixel comet streaks across the upper right. A dashed mint orbit ring curves around the lower left with a single chunky planet riding it. Both sit behind the receipt.

border:2px dashed rgba(149,215,174,.5); border-radius:50%;
▶ RIGHT-COLUMN BG

Localized starfield

8 radial-gradient star points behind the receipt only — keeps the left column readable on paper while the right column already feels like deep space. The viewer's eye does the math.

background: radial-gradient( 1.5px 1.5px at 8% 12%, rgba(149,215,174,.7) 50%, transparent 50%) /* …8 stops… */;
