05 · Who we are

Why We Founded The Counter

We founded The Counter because we felt this discomfort ourselves. We spent years at one of the world's largest innovation and technology consultancies (if you're reading, no offense), and became AI fluent largely on our own by teaching ourselves to think and work with AI on our own work until it was instinctive.

Once we got there, we could see what fluency actually makes possible. Not just doing our work faster, but doing new things we couldn't do before. And we could see how far most organizations are from giving their people the chance to get there. Most are still teaching literacy and leaving fluency to chance, and the talking points have been hammered home to the point that people are disengaging.

The window to change that is open now, but it won't stay open forever. It takes honesty about what people are feeling, patience with a process that moves one person, one problem, one solution at a time. And equally important, brave leaders who feel that the current approach isn't enough and are willing to do something about it.

We wrote down why we think this way:

Our Point of View

Stop teaching AI.
Start working with it.

  • 01 You may be investing in AI literacy when what you really need is AI fluency.
  • 02 Fluency isn’t something you can sit through. It’s built in the work itself, the same way any real capability is.
  • 03 The real opportunity is not just doing today’s work faster — it is discovering work your business could not do before.
